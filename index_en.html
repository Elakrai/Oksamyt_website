Our services

Tailoring

In our atelier you can make an order for sewing a new dress, suit, skirt, pants, national clothes according to individual sketches. We work with both our own fabric and the customer's fabric. Accessories are selected at the request of the client. We accept orders for sewing bed linen, mattresses, curtains and tulle.

Repair

We offer various types of clothing repair. At the request of the customer and in a short time. We repair and adjust both new clothes, and clothes which were in the use and clean. We establish buttons, hooks and other accessories. We change runners in zippers.

Sale

We sell various kinds of fabrics, zippers, ribbons, lace, adhesive and fabric insulation, and foam rubber. We produce and sell national clothes, Christmas costumes, pillows and bed linen. We sew covers of different sizes and purposes, standard and non-standard sizes. We cover buttons from fabric of the customer.

Sale of materials

Also, we sell haberdashery and accessories for sewing and needlework, making various creative crafts. We offer tools for knitting, embroidery, sewing, weaving: fasteners, zippers, runners, buttons, needles, needles, threads, buttons, ribbons, lace, Velcro, hoops, hoops, elastic bands and other stores.
